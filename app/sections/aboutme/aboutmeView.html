About Me

I am both passionate and curious about the many ways in which technology and society interact and am excited to explore new arenas and expand my knowledge of technology and development.

My Experience

I've worn many hats in various adventures often combining interdisciplinary areas of study to push the boundaries between technology and people. Additionally, I spent my time in the Navy ROTC developing leadership skills for better, more efficient and communicative work styles. I've learned to pick things up quickly, keep an open mind, and indulge a healthy dosage of good-natured curiosity.
